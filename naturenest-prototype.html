About Nature Nest
The ultimate plant care app for modern gardeners. Provides education, AI plant identification, expert chat, care guides, and community — helping plant owners of all skill levels keep their plants thriving.
3 User Personas
1
Busy Urbanite — automated watering reminders, quick troubleshooting
2
Novice Gardener — plant ID, beginner tutorials, personalised care
3
Plant Enthusiast — advanced diagnostics, community, smart device integration
3 Key Features
1
Plant Care Database — vast care guides with personalised recommendations
2
AI Issue Detection — snap a photo, get instant diagnosis + solutions
3
Community & Expert Chat — connect with Plantvisors and fellow growers
