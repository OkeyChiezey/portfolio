Project Overview

Connect with Fashion Stylists & Explore Trends

StyleMate was designed to solve a clear problem: people who want professional styling advice either can't afford in-person sessions, can't make time for them, or don't know where to start. The app connects users directly with vetted fashion stylists for virtual one-on-one sessions — covering wardrobe consultation, trend exploration, and personalised outfit planning.

Why it matters: Generic fashion advice is everywhere, but personalised guidance tailored to your body type, lifestyle, and existing wardrobe is rare and expensive. StyleMate brings that expertise directly to users' phones — on their schedule, at accessible price points.

The design process covered audience definition, contextual understanding, whiteboard journey mapping, wireframing, hi-fi prototyping, user interviews, and a premium membership model for sustainable monetisation.

Key Features
Browse & book vetted stylists by specialism (Casual, Formal, Seasonal, Luxury, Accessories, Makeup)
Live video chat with stylists — show your wardrobe, get real-time feedback
Schedule sessions with a built-in calendar booking system
Stylist Activity feed — latest runway picks, trend posts, seasonal advice
Premium membership: multiple sessions/month, unlimited video chat, exclusive discounts
Step 1 — Defining the Audience

Who StyleMate Was Built For

Research identified seven distinct audience segments, each with different motivations, constraints, and entry points into professional styling advice.

Fashion Enthusiasts

Always seeking new trends and ways to enhance their style. Primary power users.

Busy Professionals

Value convenience, struggle to make in-person appointments. Need scheduling flexibility.

Fashion Novices

Lack confidence in fashion choices. Need guidance for various occasions and contexts.

Life Changers

New job, wedding, major life event. Seeking a fresh wardrobe identity.

Remote Workers

WFH lifestyle. Virtual styling removes commute barrier entirely.

Gift Givers

Friends or family gifting personalized style advice for birthdays, holidays, graduations.

Special Event Attendees

Preparing for weddings, galas, or important meetings — need a guaranteed standout look.

Step 2 — User Research

Whiteboard Interview Sessions

User interviews were conducted face-to-face and recorded on a whiteboard in real time — capturing both questions and verbatim responses. The goal: understand how people currently seek styling advice, what frictions they face, and what would make a booking app genuinely useful.

Interview Introduction & Background

Participant: Marketing professional with a passion for fashion. "I seek professional styling advice a few times a year from blogs and social media, but need something more tailored for my events and wardrobe." Booked 3 sessions so far — onboarding was smooth and straightforward.

Key Feature Feedback & Suggestions

"The video chat was definitely the most useful — it let me show my wardrobe and get instant feedback." Suggested feature: ability to save style recommendations from each session directly in-app without needing to screenshot or revisit chat history.

Key Interview Quotes
Q: How often do you seek professional styling advice?
"I usually seek professional styling advice a few times a year from blogs and social media, but I need something more tailored for my specific events and wardrobe."
Q: What features did you find most useful?
"The video chat feature was definitely the most useful. It allowed me to have a real-time conversation with the stylist, show them my wardrobe and get instant feedback and recommendations."
Q: Thoughts on the Premium membership?
"I think the Premium membership offers great value, especially for users who are serious about improving their style. Having the flexibility to book multiple sessions and access unlimited video chat can really enhance the overall experience."
Q: Any feature suggestions?
"One feature I would like to see is the ability to save style recommendations from each session — not having to screenshot or go back to the chat. Overall I've had a positive experience. It's a convenient way to get professional advice without having to leave the house."
User Journey Mapping

Sarah's Journey Through StyleMate

The whiteboard journey map tracked persona Sarah through her complete interaction with StyleMate — from first contact to confirmed booking. Each stage was sketched live during the research session, capturing both the screen structure and the emotional context at each touchpoint.

The journey revealed four critical moments: discovery via social media, registering and building a style profile, discovering services and stylist guides that spoke to her wardrobe, finding a stylist that matched her aesthetic, and ultimately confirming her first session booking.

This mapping directly informed the navigation structure — why Home, Search, Schedule, Notifications, Profile, and Chat are the six core screens in the final wireframes.

Key Learnings

What the Research Taught Us

01
Seamless Onboarding
Registration must require minimal user effort. Clear instructions and minimal form fields streamline the first impression — users interviewed confirmed the current onboarding felt smooth and intuitive.
02
Transparent Feature Communication
Clearly communicate which features are gated behind premium. Users need to understand the value proposition before they're willing to upgrade — ambiguity causes drop-off.
03
Intuitive Navigation
Navigation must be logical and labelled clearly. The whiteboard wireframes helped validate that 6 core screens (Home, Search, Schedule, Notifications, Profile, Chat) cover all primary user needs without overloading the UI.
04
Personalised Recommendations
Tailor content and stylist suggestions to user style preferences and behaviour. Users who feel the app "gets them" are significantly more likely to book a second session.
05
Secure Payment Process
Trust is critical in a booking app. Robust encryption, clear pricing display, and multiple payment methods must all be in place before launch to avoid abandonment at checkout.
06
Save Session Recommendations
Top user request from interviews: the ability to save and revisit style recommendations from each session in-app — without screenshotting or trawling chat history. A future priority feature.
