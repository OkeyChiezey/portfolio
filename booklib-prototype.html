About BookLib
A social reading app where users create & join book clubs, track reading progress, vote in book polls, and discover new reads — all in one place. Inspired by the concept of discovery.
Core Flow
1
Splash → Onboarding (reading level, genres)
2
Home — Trending, Currently Reading, Book Clubs
3
Book Club — Activity feed, Books, Create Poll
4
Search — Browse by genre, find books & clubs
5
Poll — Vote & see live results
6
Create Club → Notifications → Profile
