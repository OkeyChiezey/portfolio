About StyleMate
A stylist booking app connecting users with professional fashion stylists for virtual 1-on-1 sessions — wardrobe advice, trend exploration, and outfit planning from anywhere.
User Flow
1
Splash → Log in with email or social
2
Explore categories (Casual, Formal, Luxury…)
3
Browse & save stylists → Book a session
4
Schedule — pick date, time, number of sessions
5
Confirmation + chat with stylist via video
6
Notifications · Profile & membership upgrade
Design System
Black #0A0806
Red #E81C1C
Yellow #FFD600
White #FAFAF8
Research Method
Live whiteboard user interviews — real questions, verbatim answers captured. Journey mapping, low-fi wireframing, and hi-fi Figma screens all derived from interview insights.
