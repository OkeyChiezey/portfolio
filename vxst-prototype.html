Project
VXST — Stock Investment App
A mobile-first fintech app enabling diaspora investors to buy Nigerian stocks simply — from Dangote to Paystack — with a clean, low-friction UX.
Core User Flow
1
Splash → Auth — Onboarding with Login or Sign Up
2
Home — Balance, recent transactions, referral
3
Invest — Browse top stocks, portfolio view
4
Stock Detail — Price, stats, company info
5
Enter Amount → Summary → PIN
6
Confirmation — Investment placed ✓
Design System
Colors drawn from branding guide:
Background — #B8B2CB
Surface — #3D3660
Error — #FF2D2D
UX Problem
Diaspora investors find existing Nigerian stock apps overwhelming — information overload, complex navigation, and non-friendly interfaces. VXST simplifies the entire journey to 4 taps.
